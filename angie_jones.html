Angie Jones

"Master Innovator"

About

Angie Jones attended Tennessee State University where she gained her Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. She later earned her Master's Degree in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. Her career in the tech industry began in 2003, when she worked as a software engineer at IBM for 9 years. In 2007, Jones developed and became the CEO of Diva Chix, an online game that aims at teaching teenage girls and women how to succeed in technological areas in addition to obtaining guidance in other key life lessons related to entrepreneurship, all while competing in fashion battles. As a result of her many patented inventions and her involvement in advancing the tech world, Jones has been awarded the title of IBM Master Inventor.

Currently, Jones is the Global Vice President of Developer Relations for TBD, under Block's new business unit that focuses on decentralizing technologies. She has become an award-winning teacher who continues to share her abundance of knowledge all over the world as an international keynote speaker. Her patented inventions has grown to more than 25, belonging to areas of metaverses, collaboration software, social networking, smarter planet, and software development processes.
